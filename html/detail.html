Stephen Hawking's "A Brief History of Time" is a global bestseller, translated into thirty languages and selling over ten million copies worldwide. First published in 1988, the book explores cutting-edge ideas about the universe. Over the past two decades, advancements in observing the micro and macrocosmic worlds have ushered in a new golden age for cosmology and theoretical sciences. Professor Hawking has played a key role in this intellectual renaissance.
