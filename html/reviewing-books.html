The Psychology of Money

Author: Morgan Housel

Genre: Comic

Available: 20

Rating: 4.5

Book description

Money is everywhere, it affects us all, and confuses most of us. People think about it in slightly different ways. It offers lessons that can be applied to many areas of life, such as risk, confidence, and happiness. Few topics provide a more powerful lens into why people behave the way they do than money. It's one of the most epic shows in the world.

We rarely find ourselves in a situation of being in debt? Know how to save for times of hardship? Ready for retirement? Are there more practical views on the relationship between money and our happiness?

We all do crazy things with money, because we're all new to the game, and what seems crazy to you might make sense to me. But no one is crazy – we all make decisions based on our own unique experiences that seem reasonable to us at any given time.
